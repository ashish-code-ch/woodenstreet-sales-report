📋 Coaching Playbook — Closing
Script for agents to improve their closing attempt score
The Problem
Agents present product details and then wait for the customer to make a decision. Most customers say "I'll think about it" and never call back.
The Fix — Always anchor a next step
"Can I book a store visit for you this Saturday at 11 AM?"
"Let me have our local store manager call you tomorrow — what time works?"
"I'll send you the 3 best options on WhatsApp right now — which one feels closest?"

One of these 3 lines at the end of every call will dramatically improve conversion.
📋 Coaching Playbook — Needs Discovery
Questions agents must ask before pitching any product
5 Questions Before Every Pitch
1. "What room is this furniture for?" — room size matters
2. "Do you have a budget in mind?" — saves everyone time
3. "When do you need it by?" — urgency changes approach
4. "Have you seen anything you like online?" — anchors conversation
5. "Is this for your own home or a gift?" — changes product angle

Agents who ask 3+ of these score 1.5 points higher on discovery.

✅ What's Working — Reinforce These
Strong First Impressions
Team scores 6.93 on Opening and 7.19 on Communication Clarity — agents sound professional and clear. This is a base to build on. Rajeshwari, Sachin, and Abhishek are model openers to reference in training.
Store Visit Pipeline Works

Upsell Signals Are Visible
214 calls (26.8%) had a clear upsell opportunity detected by AI — customer mentioned related products, a larger order, or asked about bundles. Only a fraction were acted on. This is low-hanging revenue.
🔴 What Needs Fixing — This Month
Priority 1: Closing Attempt (5.73/10)
Every agent. Every call. Weakest dimension team-wide. Agents present well but never ask "what's the next step?" Fix: 10-minute daily roleplay on closing language. Expected impact: +0.5 on overall score, +15% store visits.
Priority 2: Customisation Not Being Sold (206 calls)
Customers asking for customisation are told it's not available — but WoodenStreet does offer it. This is a pure training failure. Every product call should include: "We also do full customisation on dimensions and finish — want me to share options?"
Priority 3: Needs Discovery Too Shallow
327 coaching flags for poor discovery. Agents jump to pitching without understanding budget or timeline. Customers who feel heard convert at 2x the rate. Train the 5-question discovery script.
Business Signal: No Store in Tier-2 Cities
19+ calls lost because no nearby store. Consistent signals from Andhra Pradesh, specific Tier-2 cities. This is a store expansion signal — route to leadership decision.
